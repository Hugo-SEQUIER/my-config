# Asset Scoring Rubric

Total: 100 points.

## Business Quality — 20
- Revenue durability: 0–5
- Margin structure: 0–5
- Customer value / mission criticality: 0–5
- Business model clarity: 0–5

## Financial Strength — 20
- Revenue growth quality: 0–5
- Profitability / FCF: 0–5
- Balance sheet strength: 0–5
- Capital efficiency / dilution discipline: 0–5

## Market Opportunity — 15
- Market growth: 0–5
- TAM / expansion runway: 0–5
- Structural tailwinds: 0–5

## Competitive Advantage — 15
- Moat / differentiation: 0–5
- Switching costs / ecosystem: 0–5
- Competitive intensity resilience: 0–5

## Valuation — 15
- Absolute valuation reasonableness: 0–5
- Relative valuation vs peers: 0–5
- Margin of safety: 0–5

## Catalysts — 10
- Near-term catalysts: 0–4
- Medium/long-term catalysts: 0–4
- Catalyst visibility: 0–2

## Risk Control — 5
- Risk/reward asymmetry: 0–2
- Red flag absence: 0–2
- Thesis falsifiability: 0–1

## Suggested Status Mapping
- 80–100: `IN` candidate if valuation/risk are acceptable.
- 65–79: `Watchlist` or small position candidate.
- 45–64: `Watchlist` only; needs stronger evidence or better valuation.
- 0–44: `SKIP`, unless special situation.

Never use the score alone as a buy/sell decision.
